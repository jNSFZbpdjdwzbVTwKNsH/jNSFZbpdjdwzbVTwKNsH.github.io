17/01

I'm thinking about writing to Estina and inviting her over to have dinner together. I think I haven't moved on, because I've come up with the idea and I don't think it's all that pushie. I mean we haven't talk since quite some time. I am a little on the edge about this, I don't know which side it is better for me to fall on.
